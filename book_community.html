Find your book community?

Looking for a book club? Whether you want to connect over brunch or booze, explore history, cozy up with a mystery, explore other cultures, become more mindful, or find support, there's a club out there for you.

About our community

In times of stress and pressurized news agenda, many people are looking for a safe and calm space. This can be a book club: discussing literature distracts you from your anxious reality and helps you gather a community of interest. A book club is a place free from judgment, evaluation, imposition of opinions and competition.

How we do things?

We chat in a chat room on Telegram. We'll call you there after we receive your request. We have a book discussion about once a month on Saturday from 11:00 am to 1:00 pm online. In addition to books, we sometimes meet and discuss movies or TV series on the Club's topics.

Join a supportive community of people who care about the environment and read with us. We choose books to read together, as long as they are in line with the goals of sustainable development (sustainable consumption, reducing inequality, sustainable cities, etc.). Every 4-5 weeks we meet to discuss what we have read and ask questions of experts.

The book club is open to everyone regardless of immersion in the topic of frugal consumption and sustainable development. The only condition is to participate and spread the knowledge gained among friends, family, colleagues, and subscribers.
