Club Reviews

Katy

The Now So Book Club is a whole universe of like-minded people who are on a path of careful consumption. I like the club because at the meetings we share our real emotions from the book we read. Gently and gently we discuss different points of view. Brainstorm and learn from the experiences of others. After the discussion, one is filled with even more energy to accomplish feats of careful living.

Natasha

Book club motivates me to read more. I like that we choose books democratically - whichever one wins, we read that one. I also love that I have developed a habit of writing down thoughts and questions that come up while reading that I want to discuss with the club. It makes the reading process more thoughtful. And of course, I just love the atmosphere in our cozy chat room.

Ustya

In the book club I like the friendly atmosphere, mutual exchange of impressions, knowledge and useful links to movies, books and articles. The club gives an opportunity to delve into the topics that are touched upon in the books and discuss them with other members.

Indira

It just so happened that the Book Club was spun off from the Careful Consumption Club, and I migrated from there as well. I'm very glad it happened that way. I never liked reading books, but the club opened up a world of eco-themed books for me. Now I read more than ever! Sometimes it is very hard to find time to read even a book I have already chosen, but thanks to the club I always have time, and the club will always help me with the choice of literature.

Lena

I came to the club for a community of people with similar views, and also for books, because I caught myself that I started to read only fiction, and I would like to read serious non-fiction books too. For me, the club is a motivation to read and read thoughtfully, not superficially. And also a club meeting is a nice event in my calendar that I can look forward to .

Katya

Over the past year, the book club has become the most enjoyable event for me in the field of conscious consumption: it's a pleasant socializing in a community of like-minded people, discussing interesting books, and communicating with experts in a variety of fields. The club has gathered a lot of amazing interesting people from various spheres and I am glad to be acquainted with them. And meetings to discuss books I've read always reveal something new and useful, even when it seems that the book is not interesting at all.

Asya

The book club gives me a sense of a community of like-minded and like-hearted people, it's very valuable to me. I would definitely recommend joining the club to those who lack the feeling of a comrade's shoulder . I also find it difficult to concentrate on one and the same thing, and I often “atomize” my attention to many different books and other sources of information, and the club helps me to deepen my knowledge in the same area, to explore it from different angles.
