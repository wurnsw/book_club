Participating in a book club motivates you to read more often and on a regular basis, and helps you broaden your horizons.

It is a good opportunity to meet new people, discuss your favorite books, look at them in a new way, and also to learn to be brave enough to express your opinion.

Another advantage of a book club is the sharing of experience and knowledge among its participants.

Through reading books together, we create a community of like-minded people around us, even though their age, gender, profession and literary taste may differ.
